ðŸ”´ Critical Gaps Blocking Framework

1. No Historical Index Backfill HIGH

LFI, LCI, CLG, MRI all have single-point values. Without history:

  • Can't backtest signals against market outcomes
  • Can't establish regime thresholds empirically
  • Can't show "this is the worst LFI since 2008" context

Fix: Run compute_all_indicators() across full historical range and populate lighthouse_indices table.

2. Missing ISM PMI Data HIGH

ISM Manufacturing (49.3) and ISM Services (54.1) are referenced constantly in analysis but not in database. Only MANEMP (ISM Manufacturing Employment) exists.

Fix: ISM data requires subscription or scraping. Consider ISM via FRED (NAPM, NAPMNOI) or third-party API.

3. Missing NFIB Small Business Optimism HIGH

NFIB Optimism (89.4, "lowest since 2012") is a core BCI input but absent from database.

Fix: FRED series NFIB or direct from NFIB survey.

4. No Stablecoin/Crypto Data MEDIUM

SLI (Stablecoin Liquidity Impulse) exists as index but no underlying stablecoin market cap series. Critical for the crypto-Treasury transmission thesis.

Fix: Integrate CoinGecko, DefiLlama, or Glassnode API for USDT/USDC market cap.

5. Missing Temp Help Services MEDIUM

Temp Help YoY% (-7.1%) is a core labor canary but TEMPHELPS not found in series_meta.

Fix: FRED series TEMPHELPS (Temporary Help Services employment).

6. Missing 5Y5Y Forward Inflation MEDIUM

Fed's preferred inflation expectations anchor. Have 5Y and 10Y breakevens but not the 5Y5Y forward.

Fix: FRED series T5YIFR (5-Year, 5-Year Forward Inflation Expectation Rate).

What's Working Well

  • Core labor data: JOLTS rates (quits, hires, openings), claims, unemployment rates all present
  • Inflation components: CPI breakdown (shelter, food, energy, services), PCE, sticky CPI
  • Credit spreads: HY OAS, IG OAS with full history
  • Financial conditions: NFCI, Adjusted NFCI, OFR FSI components
  • Treasury curve: Full curve from 1M to 30Y plus TIPS
  • Plumbing basics: RRP, Reserves, SOFR, EFFR, TGA all present
  • Delinquencies: Comprehensive breakdown by loan type
  • horizon_dataset: ~300 pre-computed transformations (YoY, MoM, z-scores)

Bottom Line

The database is ~75% complete for the 10-Pillar framework. The core foundation is solidâ€”labor flows, inflation components, credit spreads, plumbing metrics all present. But we're missing critical survey data (ISM, NFIB, Conference Board) that's referenced constantly in analysis, and our proprietary indices have no historical depth.

The highest-leverage fix is backfilling the proprietary indices. Right now we can say "LFI is +0.93" but we can't say "that's the highest since 2008." Context requires history.

Second priority: ISM and NFIB. These are quoted in every Beacon and Horizon. Having them computed externally and pasted in defeats the purpose of the infrastructure.
