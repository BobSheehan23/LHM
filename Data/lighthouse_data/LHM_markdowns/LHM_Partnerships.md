### Strategic Context

The Allocation Innovation Partnership (AIP) represents a paradigm shift in institutional intelligence infrastructure — converting allocator behavior into quantifiable macro variables through AI-integrated workflows.

### LHM's Contribution Architecture

#### Analytical Engine Integration
**Core Deliverables:**
- Liquidity Transmission Framework (LTF) integration
- Treasury market stress diagnostics
- Macro-behavioral signal development
- Capital flow quantification methodologies

**Value Proposition:**
- Transform intuitive allocator decisions into systematic signals
- Bridge macro dynamics with micro-behavioral patterns

**Pre-Existing IP:**
- LHM retains ownership of core frameworks (LTF, Capital Sprints™)

- Attribution requirements for LHM-sourced analytics

**Joint Development:**
- Co-ownership of newly developed frameworks
- Revenue share on AIP products utilizing LHM methodologies

**Scope:** Co-develop quantification methodology for allocator behavior

**Deliverables:**

**Compensation Model:**
