# Lighthouse Macro Business Strategy & Revenue Architecture

## Executive Summary

This document articulates Lighthouse Macro's multi-channel revenue generation strategy, designed to capture institutional value while democratizing sophisticated macro intelligence. The architecture balances high-margin institutional services with scalable subscription products, creating a defensible $1M+ ARR business within 18 months.

- Gather feedback and testimonials
- Refine value proposition

- Document conversion metrics

#### Phase 3: Public Launch (Weeks 5-8)
- Coordinated multi-channel announcement
- Limited-time founding rate ($500 vs $750)

- Social proof amplification
- Referral incentive program

#### Phase 4: Momentum Sustaining (Ongoing)
